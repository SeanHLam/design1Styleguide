Browny the Raccoon

Logo Mark & Logo Type

Our logo mark is based off our mascot, Browny. The feeling we wanted to go for was "relaxed".

The logo type pairs well with this theme with it's rounded edges.

What does it communicate?

The logo uses soft curved lines which adds to the relaxed feel. Seeing them content also makes the user content.

The soft font lets users know that MindFull is taking it easy.

Key Design Principles

The movement of the logo directs the user upwards to the mind - the central theme of MindFull

Browny himself is symmetrical while the three stars balance each other out making for a balanced logo.

Alternate Sizing

Our default logo size is 150px by 150px. Our horizantal version is wider at 300px by 150px.

The logo can also be downscaled for things like our nav bar where it sits at 60px by 60px.

Clearspace & Icon sizing

The logo's clearspace is 40px on each side.

Icons are the smallest version of the Logo and are sized at 20px by 20px.
