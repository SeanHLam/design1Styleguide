Typography

As seen below, sans-serif fonts were in favor to convey simplicity and minimalism.

Patrick Hand - Header / Logo Text

Handwritten font that overall gives off a playful and friendly feeling, thus being a good fit. The font is certainly and easily readable for users of any age despite being handwritten.

Header Samples
"What type of personality do you have?"
"No matter what you are, we can all struggle with our mental health"

DM Sans - Content / Body Text

A sans-serif font mostly intended for use at small text sizes such as content and body text. It pairs well with our header font which was the main priority.

Content Text Samples
"Your view on mental health can be influenced by the media you consume. Bad depictions form stigmas about mental illnesses..."
"Physical and mental health are linked to each other. People who suffer from physical ailments are more likely..."

Oswald - Button Text

Clean, bold sans-serif font generally found in button texts. Its boldness helps users get a clear direction of where they are headed next.
